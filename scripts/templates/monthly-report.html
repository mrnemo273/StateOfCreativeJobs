About This Report

State of Creative Jobs tracks 20 creative and design job titles across four clusters, measuring demand, compensation, AI displacement risk, and practitioner sentiment. Data is refreshed weekly from public APIs and scored using a composite model blending O*NET task automability (40%) with an editorial Tool Displacement Index (60%).

This report is for informational purposes only and does not constitute career, financial, or investment advice. Data is sourced from third-party APIs and may not reflect all market conditions.
